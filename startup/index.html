内地：学科补习全面禁止 Mainland China: Academic Tutoring Banned

2021年7月，中国国务院发布「双减政策」，学科类校外培训遭受全面打击： In July 2021, China's State Council issued the "Double Reduction" policy, devastating the tutoring industry:

  • 义务教育阶段（1至9年级）学科补习全面禁止营利For-profit academic tutoring banned for grades 1–9
  • 周末、节假日、寒暑假不得开展学科培训No tutoring allowed on weekends, holidays, or vacations
  • 外资禁止进入学科类培训领域Foreign investment in academic tutoring is prohibited
  • 新东方、学而思等行业巨头被迫转型或关闭Industry giants like New Oriental and TAL Education forced to pivot or close

香港：教育市场合法蓬勃 Hong Kong: Legal & Thriving Education Market

作为特别行政区，香港的教育市场不受「双减」影响，补习行业持续发展： As a Special Administrative Region, Hong Kong's education market is unaffected by the "Double Reduction" policy:

  • 学科补习完全合法，受市场自由竞争驱动Academic tutoring is fully legal, driven by free market competition
  • 紧邻大湾区，跨境学童需求持续增长Adjacent to the Greater Bay Area with growing cross-border student demand
